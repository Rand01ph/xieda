Our services. What we do.

What we do

At ISGM, we transform your workforce by offering an end-to-end managed service solution — from initial advice on the right workforce strategy to meet your company’s goals, through to implementation of your transformation agenda including the ongoing operational management of your workforce.

By working in partnership, ISGM is able to achieve a reduction in net costs, unlock innovation and efficiencies, increase customer satisfaction and create a competitive advantage.

Our proven ability to create workforce efficiencies means that we deliver:

Actual cost savings

  • A transparent financial and operational model realising significant savings through removing duplication of operating costs and associated processes.

Incentivised and engaged workforce

  • Demonstrated capability to deliver industry specific labour resources on large scale via an integrated workforce with real time performance analysis and business improvement benefits.

Increased levels of customer service

  • Well-managed workforce leading to highly motivated people with less churn, so skills remain in the business.

Real transparency for business improvement

  • A true partnership with associated alignment of outcomes, strategy and goals.

Enhanced safety

  • Safety-first culture – with certifications from AS/NZS 4801 Safety Management System, AS/NZS 9001 Quality Management System and AS/NZS 14001 Environmental Management System and Federal Safety Commission.
